For example: the number of people with diabetes, collected by the National Diabetes Audit, including the registration from primary and secondary care:

• Registrations from primary care: diabetes patients with a GP record in the selected data.
• Registrations from secondary care: diabetes patients with a secondary care record in the selected data, but not a GP record.
